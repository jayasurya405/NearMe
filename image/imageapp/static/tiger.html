The Bengal Tiger is the most numerous tiger subspecies and the national animal of India. Known for its majestic orange coat with bold black stripes, it is one of the world’s largest big cats. Bengal Tigers play a vital role in maintaining the balance of ecosystems and are a symbol of strength and courage. 🐅 Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris 🌍 Habitat: India, Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan 🎯 Unique Feature: Powerful build, sharp vision, and hunting skills 🔎 Conservation Status: Endangered (due to poaching & habitat loss)
